medi8

The goal of the medi8 project is to develop a non-linear audio/video editing suite within the Eclipse framework.

To find out more, or to get involved, check out these links:

  • The medi8-hackers mailing list archive.
  • The medi8 developer page, hosted on BerliOS.
  • Our blogs: green, tromey.

medi8 runs on Linux.
